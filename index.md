# SortItOut

A Chrome extension that helps you clean up your inbox by surfacing newsletter and promotional senders, then letting you unsubscribe or trash their emails with a swipe.

## Features

- **Local-only** - no backend, no analytics, no tracking
- Works with **Gmail** and **Outlook**
- One-click unsubscribe via the standard `List-Unsubscribe` header
- Swipe-to-trash for senders you no longer want to hear from
- Dismissed-sender list so reviewed senders don't reappear

## Privacy

SortItOut never reads message bodies, attachments, or contacts. We only access email headers (sender, `List-Unsubscribe`) and basic metadata (read/unread status, message count). All data stays on your device in `chrome.storage.local`.
